Welcome

The goal of this page is to introduce myself and highlight my work as a graduate student in the field of organizational communication.
Through sharing a bit of myself and my experience with graduate work, I hope to demystify the world of academia and encourage people to consider academic work--whether it be reading more academic articles or pursuing a graduate degree of their own. Though the primary focus will be on my work, in the future I plan to share more stories about my academic journey.
